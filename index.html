PROJECTS AND DEVELOPMENT


Incoming Communication Management System (ICMS)




Designed to streamline and organize all forms of communication received by an organization. This system captures emails, messages, and physical mail, categorizing them for easy access and tracking. It ensures timely responses, enhances collaboration among team members, and maintains a comprehensive log of interactions for future reference. The system is crucial for improving customer service and maintaining strong relationships with stakeholders.

Cemetery Infomation Management System (CIMS)




An efficient solution for managing cemetery operations. It includes features for tracking plots, burials, and maintenance activities, as well as managing reservations and sales. The system enables staff to maintain accurate records and provides families with access to information about their loved ones. Additionally, it offers tools for managing financial transactions and generating reports, ensuring smooth operations and improved service for visitors.

Sex Desigrated Community Base Management System (CBMS)




A software solution designed to monitor and manage the residents of a specific housing community, with a focus on demographic details such as sex/gender composition and household structure. This system collects and organizes data related to the composition of households, allowing local authorities or housing administrators to better understand the needs of the community, allocate resources efficiently, and make informed decisions regarding community services and infrastructure.
